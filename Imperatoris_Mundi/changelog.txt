Changelog:
	Initial Release (0.9 Beta)
		* Large Cities require 18000 population, Huge Cities require 30000 population
		* Squalor and overcrowding start to apply with larger populations
		* Distance From Capital Penalty Reduced for larger map size
		* Character, Army, Navy, and Admiral movements increased for larger map size
		* Jungle climate added (replacing temperate open)

		* MARIAN REFORMS REQUIRE THREE HUGE ITALIAN CITIES instead of vanilla's one huge italian city.
